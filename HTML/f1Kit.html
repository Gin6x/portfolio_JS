f1_Kit

Checking F1 race ranking and circuit location

This app is design for the fans of Formula one or actually just for me being lazy. You could check the ranking of Drivers and teams and also learn more about the circuits of F1 racing.
In further development plan, general race result or of specific driver race result could be display and bookmark as well.

    Technologies

  • Storyboard
  • Api call
  • MapKit
  • Swift

Ranking

By selecting from Driver and year button, user could checking the ranking for drivers and teams in different year up to 10 years.

Circuit

Showing all race circuits in currently season.
Details of the circuit and location in map would be provided.
